Boba Bliss!!!!

I enjoy learning about boba drinks because there are so many flavors, toppings, and tea bases to try. My favorite drink is brown sugar milk tea with chewy tapioca pearls. You can learn more about bubble tea on this bubble tea information page.

Fun Facts about Boba

  • The tapioca pearls are made from cassava starch.
  • Bubble tea originated in Taiwan in the 1980s.
  • There are many variations of bubble tea, including fruit teas and slushies.
